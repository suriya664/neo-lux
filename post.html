Copper Pulse Animation System

The Copper Pulse system is a set of motion guidelines for cinematic interfaces. It delivers the drama of luminous copper sweeps while honoring WCAG 2.1 motion preferences.

Why Copper?

Copper radiates warmth on darker palettes and pairs beautifully with cool industrial neutrals. Its reflective quality translates into gradients that feel tangible, guiding focus without harsh contrast.

Motion Foundations

  • Motion amplitude scales with user preference settings.
  • Directional cues follow narrative beats, never looping without purpose.
  • Ease curves mimic breathing: easeOutQuad for entrances, easeInOutCubic for transitions.

Accessibility Layer

We provide toggles for reduced motion, removing parallax and swapping to soft fades. All critical information remains visible when animations pause.
