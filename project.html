Flux Atrium Immersion

A cinematic walkthrough guiding visitors through responsive copper light, sound, and tactile experiences.

Role
Experience Design Lead, Motion Director
Year
2024
Deliverables
Interaction Blueprint, Immersive UI, Inclusive Motion System
Collaborators
Audio Alchemy, Lumens Lab
View Live Showcase

Objective

Create an immersive atrium that reacts to visitor motion, choreographing light, projection, and ambient audio within a copper industrial space.

Approach

We prototyped in Unreal Engine and TouchDesigner, crafting a dynamic narrative that shifts through copper-toned phases. Accessibility toggles allowed sensory-friendly light and motion presets.

Impact

Visitors spent 40% longer inside the installation, citing emotional resonance and comfort. The project received the Nocturne Experiential Award 2024.

Gallery

Step-by-step

01 · Atmosphere Mapping

3D scans of the venue informed the copper beam placements and projected pathways.

02 · Motion System

GSAP-driven interface prototypes allowed visitors to adjust intensity with accessible presets.

03 · Live Calibration

We staged sensory walkthroughs with diverse visitors, tuning motion softness and light rhythm.

04 · Launch Immersion

Captured cinematic stills and interactive clips that powered the launch campaign and investor preview decks.
